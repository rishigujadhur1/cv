How we work

Digital marketing is an iterative process, and regardless of the project, we remain by your side to
continuously improve your efforts and deliver the best results.
Situational analysis

The first thing we do is carrying out an internal and external analysis (SWOT analysis) of the firm to find out its strengths and weaknesses.

Marketing goals establishment

Once you have your strong points in mind, we’ll work on establishing some goals to have a clear idea of where your actions should take you.

Defining the marketing strategy

Based on our objectives, we’ll define & carry out different strategies: email marketing campaigns, social media, CRM, web optimization, SEO etc.

Measuring results

We have to measure every action using KPIs to figure out if we earned the ROI we expected and achieved the goals we set.

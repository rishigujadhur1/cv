Easily connect your headphones

With an exclusive Lintense app for iOS and Android, you can automatically connect the headphones to your favorite devices.

Pair your Lintense headphones

Lintense headphones can remember up to 3 favorite devices and easily pair with them. To change an active device, just press a square button on your headphones.

Disconnect or reconnect

Need to disconnect & connect Lintense headphones to another device? No problem! Just press an “L” button on the headphones. It will reset the previous connection.

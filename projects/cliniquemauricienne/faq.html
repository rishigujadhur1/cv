General Information

What time do doctors visit?
There is no set time. The doctors come between their consultations. Your nurse can contact them at any time if necessary.
Should I make an appointment at the doctor?
Your physician will tell you on the same day as the intervention what procedure must be followed for the rest of your treatment (appointment with your doctor, sick leave, etc ...).
What time can I leave the Clinic the day of the departure?
The time you leave is set by the doctor who treated you or by the anaesthetist who took care of you (this is with regards to outpatients).
Can one consult someone at the Clinic?
There are no consulting doctors at the Clinic but you will find a list of accredited doctors per specialisation under the DOCTORS section on our home page. In the event of an emergency, please contact or meet at our Emergency Centre which is open from Monday to Friday from 7am to 9pm and on Saturdays and Sundays from 9am to 9pm.
